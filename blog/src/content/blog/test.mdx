## Smart Quotes and Apostrophes

Here’s a test of apostrophes:  
**You’re**, **It’s**, **Don’t**, **We’ll**, **I’m**  

Smart double quotes:  
> “This is a test of curly quotes.”

Smart single quotes:  
> ‘This is a test of single curly quotes.’

Normal ASCII equivalents:  
> "This is normal double quote"  
> 'This is normal single quote'

Dashes:  
– En dash  
— Em dash
