### 🚀 Why Staking on aptcore.one?

Staking APT not only earns you passive income but also contributes to the security and decentralization of the Aptos blockchain.

Using Petra Wallet with aptcore.one ensures:
- 🔒 Secure connection & transaction signing
- 🎯 Optimized for desktop & mobile

## ✅ Steps Covered in the Video

1. Install and set up [Petra Wallet](https://petra.app/)
2. Visit [aptcore.one](https://aptcore.one)
3. Click “Connect Wallet”
4. Approve connection in Petra
5. Stake your APT and track rewards!
